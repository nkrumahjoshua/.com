The Artist

About Justina

Justina Valentine is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, TV host and model from Passaic County, New Jersey. Raised in a family of musicians, she studied dance and worked in theatre before channelling everything into hip-hop and R&B.

She launched her career in 2012 on the Vans Warped Tour, dropping her debut mixtape Route 80. Her 2015 collab with Fetty Wap, Candy Land, put her on the map worldwide. The 2016 album Scarlet Letter and mixtape Feminem cemented her as a force to be reckoned with.

Best known as a cast member on MTV's Wild 'N Out alongside Nick Cannon since 2016, Justina is known for her razor-sharp freestyle, bold red hair, and zero-compromise attitude.
